Happy Farmers

In 2013, India earned $26 billion from $276 billion trade in commercial services. Whereas agricultural trade of $71 billion fetched as much as $23 billion trade surplus. This shows that the net foreign earnings from agriculture are at par with the IT sector.

Indian farmers don multiple hats. Other than tending to their crops, they also take on the role of mechanic, weatherman, veterinarian, businessman, marketeer and scientist.

According to the data from the agriculture ministry, India produced a whopping 95.9 million tons of wheat in the year 2013-14.

Organic farming in India is largely based on traditional farming method that have been passed down since centuries, and is "as organic as it gets.
